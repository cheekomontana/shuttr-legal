Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Shuttr ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Shuttr mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address to create and manage your account. You may also use the app without creating an account.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including the number of image and video generations and credits used.
  • Photos & Prompts: Photos you upload and text prompts you enter are sent to third-party AI services for processing. We do not permanently store your uploaded photos on our servers after processing is complete.

2. Photos & AI Processing

When you use our AI editing features, your photos (which may include faces) and text prompts are sent to third-party AI services for real-time processing:

  • Image Generation: Photos and prompts are sent to Google's Gemini API (operated by Google DeepMind) for AI image generation and editing.
  • Video Generation: Photos and prompts are sent to xAI's Grok API (operated by xAI) for AI video generation.

We do not extract, store, or perform biometric analysis on facial features. No facial recognition, face mapping, or facial geometry data is collected at any point. Photos are processed in real-time and are not retained on our servers beyond the generation session. Generated results may be saved to your device or account history if you are signed in.

Third-Party Face Data Handling: Neither Google (Gemini API) nor xAI (Grok API) store your face data or uploaded photos. Photos containing faces are transmitted via encrypted connection, processed in real-time to generate your requested result, and immediately discarded by these services upon completion of the request. No face data is retained, extracted, or stored by any third party at any point. For details on how these providers handle data, please refer to Google's AI terms and xAI's terms of service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To manage your account and credits
  • To process your AI image and video generation requests via third-party AI services
  • To communicate important updates about the service

4. Third-Party AI Services

We use the following third-party AI services to process your generation requests:

  • Google Gemini API (Google DeepMind): Receives user-uploaded photos and text prompts for AI image generation. Data is processed in real-time according to Google's AI terms (https://ai.google.dev/terms). Google does not store uploaded photos or face data beyond the processing request.
  • xAI Grok API (xAI): Receives user-uploaded photos and text prompts for AI video generation. Data is processed in real-time according to xAI's terms of service (https://x.ai/legal/terms-of-service). xAI does not store uploaded photos or face data beyond the processing request.

These services receive only the data necessary for processing (your photo and prompt). We do not share any other personal information with these services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • AI Service Providers: Google (Gemini API) and xAI (Grok API) for processing AI generation requests as described above.
  • Infrastructure Providers: Supabase for authentication and data storage, Cloudflare for API routing.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain your original uploaded photos or face data. AI processing occurs in real-time, and photos are not stored on our servers after processing. Our third-party AI providers (Google and xAI) also do not retain uploaded photos or face data beyond the duration of each generation request. Account data (email, credit balances, generation history) is retained until you delete your account.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You may delete your account at any time from the app's Settings page. This will permanently remove your account data from our systems.

9. Children's Privacy

Shuttr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@shutterai.app.
